Why this matters

Most revenue leakage is not dramatic. It is quiet, repeatable, and easy to miss.

In complex spine and orthopedic cases, modifier nuance and documentation details often get reconstructed too late. By then, the missed opportunity is harder to recover and harder to defend.

Missed coding opportunities

Cases can be broadly correct while still missing add-on logic, modifiers, or supporting detail that materially affects reimbursement.

Too much downstream cleanup

When issues surface after submission, your team gets pulled into clarification loops, denials, and manual rework that should have been avoided upstream.

No clear pattern visibility

Without a structured review, it is hard to see where the same leakage keeps repeating across surgeons, procedures, and documentation habits.
