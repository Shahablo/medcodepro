Why this matters

Most revenue leakage is not dramatic. It is quiet, repeatable, and easy to miss.

Complex cases, modifier nuance, and documentation details often get reconstructed too late. By then, the missed opportunity is already harder to recover.

Missed coding opportunities

Cases can be billed generally correctly while still missing add-on logic, modifiers, or supporting details that affect reimbursement.

Too much downstream cleanup

When issues surface after submission, teams get dragged into clarification loops, denials, and manual rework.

No clear pattern visibility

Without a structured review, it is hard to know where the same leakage keeps repeating across cases and providers.
