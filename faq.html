FAQ

Questions physicians and practices are likely to ask.

Who is MedCode Pro built for?

MedCode Pro is built for surgeons and practices dealing with coding complexity, starting with orthopedics and spine.

Does this replace my billing team?

No. It is designed to strengthen the coding signal upstream and help reduce missed value and rework downstream.

Is the product focused only on revenue?

No. The product should increase coding confidence while staying grounded in documentation and compliance-aware review.

Can it support documentation quality too?

Yes. One of the most important functions is helping physicians see what needs to be documented to support the coding path.

Can I see the product before committing?

Yes. The best next step is a short demo tailored to your workflow and specialty use cases.
